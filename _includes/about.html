Research Interests

I am broadly interested in building efficient and scalable computer systems, given emerging applications including machine learning and visualization, with multiple perspectives: compiler, computer architecture, and algorithm. In particular, I am very interested in the problems of optimizing systems for ML algorithms as well as designing ML algorithms for systems and given resources. Additionally, I feel excited to try applying various conceptual models from different fields of study while solving the problems. My recent work focused on automatically searching for convolutional neural network structures that could be deployed on edge devices while maximizing the utilization of available resources.
