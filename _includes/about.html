Mobile-efficient NAS for Vision Tasks



  • Proposed a Neural Architecture Search(NAS) to search for mobile-efficient ConvNets.
  • Searched models maintain similar or higher accuracy and half the latency to SOTA ConvNets.

  • Used:
    • Model Specification, Training: PyTorch, Python
    • Model Deployment, Optimization: TVM Relay, Python, C++
